WHY OUR APP WILL ADDRESS THIS ISSUE

Following LASPO, most housing cases outside homelessness, disrepair or possession were removed from the scope of legal aid.

This means there is a fundamental power imbalance between tenants and landlords, especially in areas of housing shortages, creating competition amongst tenants who feel they must agree to unfair rental hikes from Landlords

Our app aims to ‘fill the gap’ for those who do not fall in the latter category risking homelesness or eviction, but for those struggling with rental increases that are considered unfair.

This is an issue because:

  • 15% of tenants have not challenged an unfair rent increase
  • Tenants fear retaliatory eviction: 1 in 8 tenants have not challenged a rent increase because they fear eviction.
